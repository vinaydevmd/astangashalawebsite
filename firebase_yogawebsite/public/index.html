Namaste

As a Divine blessing, I received the practice of yoga at an young age, and have been a sincere practitioner of its principles ever since. I completed the second sequences of Ashtanga vinyasa yoga. Eventually, was initiated into Meditation, which is the main practice of Ashtanga Yoga.

I am teaching to sincerely share the same. 

One of the great thinkers once said when asked "Who are you?" to which the reply was "That is what I am thinking". This quest has been the road to walk, the practice for me, to reveal its path. I realise Yoga is a practice of befriending our mind, so that life can be in peace, and living at ease.
